Web designer. Web Developer. Graphic Designer. World-Champion Selfie Taker.

My interest in web design began way back in the late nineties when I acquired my first computer and spent far too much time tying up my phone line to browse the internet. I earned a degree in web design in 2002, which led to a career that includes teaching web design and development, starting up and working for design firms, and working in a large corporate environment. Over the last 16 years I’ve designed and built all sorts of websites for a wide variety of industries.

When I began building websites, the average internet user squinted at browsers on boxy beige monitors with tiny glass screens. Today I specialize in building mobile-first responsive web designs optimized for viewing on those magic glass rectangles we can’t seem to stop staring at. I will continue to design and build websites until someone tells me to stop, and I look forward to the day we finally find out what the internet smells like (my guess is bananas).

Brightwood College Bootstrap Rebuild

ECA’s main school brand websites were redesigned with the help of an agency and launched on the Sitecore platform in 2016. After a couple of years of using the platform, the team decided the Sitecore platform wasn’t the best fit for us, considering our small size. With approval from the CMO and VP of Marketing, the team set out at the beginning of 2018 to faithfully recreate these sites using Bootstrap 4 as the front-end framework, with a custom ColdFusion CMS for managing the content on the backend. Using tools and technology the team was more comfortable using, we were able to recreate the sites with greater flexibility, faster deployment possibilities, and easier maintenance. Brightwood, being our largest and best lead-generating brand, was developed first.

Brightwood College Flexible Landing Page

As ECA developed relationships with third party vendors to secure Adwords and display advertising, the team developed a flexible, database-driven landing page template to allow for rapid deployment of customized landing page experiments for our various advertising experiments. Through our CMS we could rapidly deploy a landing page with customized content, sometimes in as little as half an hour. In addition to customizing the content, we could adjust the fields on the form to provide options for all campuses and programs, a single campus, or a single program.
